Bangers and Mash Potatoes

"Bangers & Mash" by Annie Mole is licensed under CC BY 2.0 .

Description

Mashed potatoes and fried sausages with optional gravy

Ingredients

  • 500grams of sausages
  • Potatoes
  • butter
  • gravy

Steps

Prepare sausages
  1. Heat up oil
  2. Fry sausages till brown
Prepare Mashed potatoes
  1. Peel potatoes and boil till soft
  2. Mash with dash of milk, 150g of butter, with salt and pepper
Enjoy
