Salmon on Toast

"Favorite breakfast for my birthday: Bi-rite house-smoked salmon on toasted sliced sourdough." by ecastro is licensed under CC BY 2.0 .

Description

Smoked salmon on toast with cracked pepper and lemon juice

Ingredients

  • Smoked salmon
  • Bread
  • Cracked pepper
  • lemon
  • butter

Steps

Prepare Salmon
  1. Bread in toaster
  2. Spread butter on once out of toaster
  3. squirt lemon juice on toast
  4. Add cracked pepper
  5. Put salmon on the toast
Enjoy
